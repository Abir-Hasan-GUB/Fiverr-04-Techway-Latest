Business Analysis Work Experience Program

Techway Placements has been helping aspiring business analysts position themselves for job search and career success by enabling them to build relevant work experience in the sector and role. Integrated within the program are the latest concepts and techniques that are crucial for overcoming many of the challenges common to the BA sector and profession. We offer this opportunity in partnership with different companies as well as business analysis experts within and outside the UK.

Request more Information

Program Overview

This work experience program is your window to the role of a business analyst. Develop critical business analysis experience and skills that will help support your entry into the sector and will allow you to function effectively in any traditional or agile environment.

At the heart of the program is the chance to work alongside an industry expert mentor, who will be assigning tasks and projects based on real business situations and challenges. The mentor will also provide guidance and advice throughout the program to help reinforce your strengths, expose you to the realities of working life, and enable you to get a good start in your career.

Program Coverage

As a candidate of the program, you will be working on a range of tasks and projects that are based on real business situations and data. This means you will be taking on a number of responsibilities designed to keep you engaged throughout the program while also building your BA skills and experience. Some of the key areas covered in the program include:

  • Requirements lifecycle management
  • Requirements analysis and design definition
  • Strategy analysis
  • Elicitation and collaboration
  • Planning and monitoring
  • Project management
  • Solution implementation and evaluation

Business Analysis Work Experience Reinvented

With this program, you will have the opportunity to work with an industry expert mentor. Your mentor will assign you tasks and projects that you need to complete. This ensures that in a matter of weeks you will be able to perform different BA functions on a professional level and will be fully prepared to apply for a BA role.

We are confident that this program is well worth undertaking for the following reasons:

  • No upfront fee needed; 100% free to join
