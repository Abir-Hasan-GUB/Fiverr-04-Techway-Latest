Why partner with us?

As a recruiter, you are expected by client companies to find the right people to join their teams. This is challenging given the intensifying competition for high-performing candidates. Partnering with Experiencely can help you outpace your competitors and satisfy the requirements of the clients you work with. Here’s what you can expect from us as a partner.

Access talented
graduates

Have a steady pipeline of candidates that you can readily forward for entry-level tech roles. Graduates from our programs have valuable work experience to show as well as qualifications that employers are looking for. With those, they are eager to get their start in the world of work and are prepared to hit the ground running.

Careful
vetting

When it comes to verification, requesting for a copy of a candidate’s CV is no longer enough for us. To get into any of our work experience program, our team conducts an interview. This enables us to know if candidates are suited for the program they are trying to get in and if they have the necessary skills to succeed.

Unambiguous
Communication

When you become a partner recruiter of Techway Placements, rest assured that we will communicate with you consistently. We will share the information you need about the graduates we have on board and at the same time relay the job positions you are hiring for. This ensures we are able to help candidates from both sides.

Partnership promise

We strive to give candidates a better chance at securing a role that fits their skills. Developing strategic partnerships with recruiters like yourself is our way of making that happen. We understand that being in a professional relationship like this should be a win-win situation for you and us. In that respect, we are making the following commitment so you can get the best results while working with us.

As Techway Placements' partner recruiter, our promise is to help you generate candidate leads by giving you access to our pool of graduates. This should help streamline your recruitment process, while ensuring that you have a steady pipeline of experienced and qualified entry-level candidates that you can readily place for full-time tech roles.

Support and guidance

Once you have decided to become a Techway Placements' partner recruiter, we will provide you with the support and guidance you need to set up your account and get started with your recruitment activities. You can g et in touch with our support team at anytime for any question or concern you might have.
